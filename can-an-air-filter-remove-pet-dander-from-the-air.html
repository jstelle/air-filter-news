Can an Air Filter Remove Pet Dander from The Air?

How to remove pet dander from your home?

The best way to remove pet dander from your home is to clean regularly. You can clean your carpets, rugs, upholstery, and other areas that are most likely to be covered in pet dander. There are also pet dander traps that you can place in your home to trap and remove pet dander from the air.There are pet dander traps for indoor and outdoor use. Indoor traps are great for cleaning your carpets and rugs. Outdoor traps are great for cleaning up vegetation and other outdoor sources of pet dander. Depending on the size of your home and the number of pets you have, you may need multiple traps to remove pet dander from your home.
