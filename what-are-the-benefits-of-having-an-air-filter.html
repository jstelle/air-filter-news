What Are the Benefits of Having an Air Filter?

An air filter is a device that traps dust, dirt, and other pollutants that could otherwise be released into the air from your HVAC system. It’s an essential part of any home or office HVAC system. Without an air filter, these pollutants could build up and cause a variety of negative side effects. These include allergies, irritation of the eyes and skin, and even long-term health problems such as lung damage if inhaled for extended periods. With regular maintenance, an air filter can last anywhere from one to three months depending on the type of HVAC system in use and the location of the home. If you’re in the process of installing a new HVAC system, an air filter will also ensure that your indoor air quality is at its best from day one. There are many different types of air filters available on the market, so it’s important to choose one that best suits your needs and home environment. Depending on the type of filter you select, it may trap specific contaminants such as dust, pollen, pet dander, or bacteria. These contaminants can cause serious long-term health issues if they’re inhaled regularly or over long periods of time.
