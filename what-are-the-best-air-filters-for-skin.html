What is an air filter?

An air filter is a device that traps harmful particles in the air to make it cleaner. These filters are commonly found in HVAC systems or in the vents of your house. They’re designed to trap small contaminants like dust, pet dander, bacteria, smoke, and more. They are commonly used in medical facilities and in HVAC systems. - Activated carbon filter: These filters are made from coconut husk, wood pulp, or other types of carbon. They’re often used in HVAC systems and in the home to trap odors and VOCs. - Oil-based filter: These filters are made from materials like cotton, paper, or other organic materials. They’re used in industrial settings to trap harmful chemicals.
